Our Story

Built on Craft,
Grown by Trust

Down and Dirty Landscaping was founded in 2012 by a father and son who believed that great outdoor spaces start with honest hard work. What began as a two-man operation has grown into a full-service company with a crew of 12 certified professionals.

We serve homeowners who want their outdoor space to reflect the same quality as the inside of their home — people who value craftsmanship, reliability, and a clean job site at the end of every day.
